OMELETA

Description

To make an omeleta you need 5 ingredients, a spoon, a fork and a microwave. To prepare it you also need a bowl and 10 minutes to spare.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • eggs
  • milk
  • pepper
  • salt
  • garlic powder

Steps

The steps you need to foolow are:

  1. Break the eggs
  2. Add the salt, the pepper, the milk and the garlic powder
  3. Mix them all together
  4. Cook the mixture for 10 minutes
