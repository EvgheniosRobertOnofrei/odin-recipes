Description

In order to make a pizza you will need an oven, some ingredients, a spoon, a fork and 30 minutes to spare.

Ingredients

Ingredients you will need:

  • flour
  • eggs
  • salt
  • cheese
  • salami

Steps

The steps to follow are:

  1. Mix the eggs with the flour
  2. Add the salt
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Bake the dough to the oven for 30 minutes
